<audio>
Extensions: .midi, .mid, .wma, .rm, .ram, .wav, .aac, .mp3, .ogg, .mp4. There may be more.
<audio controls>
<source src="songs.ogg" type="audio/ogg">
<source src="songs.mp3" type="audio/mpeg"> Your browser does not support the audio tag.
</audio>
