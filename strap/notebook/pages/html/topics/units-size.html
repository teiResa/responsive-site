CSS Units of Size

  • Do not use print units on screen;
  • Units can be Absolute or they are relative; and,
  • Anything in italics is hoverable.
CSS Units of Size
Col1 Col2 Col3
Screen only:
em relative Width of a capital M in used font.
px absolute {Example 1a} <div style="border-radius: 50px;">
% relative {Example 1b} <img src="" alt="" width="20%">
i am blank.
ex equal to the height of the lowercase "x".
Print only:
em relative Width of a capital M in used font.
cm, mm, in absolute They stand for centimeter, millimeter, and inches, respectively.
pt absolute At 1⁄72 of an international inch, this is typography's smallest unit of measure.
pc absolute About 1⁄6 of an inch, or 1⁄72 of a foot. A foot is 30.48 cm. So, < 0.43 cm.
% relative If parent container is 100%. 1⁄2 is 50%, 1⁄4 is 25% , 3⁄4 is 75% , etc.
